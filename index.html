WHO WE ARE
CitizenCity seeks to be Pittsburgh's only coworking space dedicated to providing collaborative space, resources & community to our city's mission-driven professionals.

We believe that a more equitable and just world requires bringing together a diverse community of people committed to social impact. Our space and programs seek to support an inclusive community of mission-driven professionals , students and neighbors .

OUR ROADMAP
In August 2015, we began having conversations and cultivating relationships with the organizations and people that make up Pittsburgh's social impact community. In 2016 we want to expand and grow our community of supporters.
Beginning January 2016, we will publish an online newsletter- The Weekly Dose of Good, which will highlight the events, people and ideas shaping Pittsburgh's social impact community.
Beginning in the spring of 2016, we will offer pop-up events around the pop-up workshops and events for Pittsburgh's social impact community.
